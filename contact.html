FAQ

What people ask us most

How long does my site take to be ready?
It depends on the plan. A landing page takes roughly 3–5 business days; a brochure site 1–2 weeks; and an e-commerce project around 3 weeks. Timelines begin once we have all your materials.
Do I need a logo or images before we start?
Not necessarily. We can guide you toward free professional image resources and work with whatever you have. Logo design can be quoted separately.
Who handles hosting and the domain?
Domain and hosting are not included in the plans. We'll help you choose the best option and walk you through setup at no extra charge.
Can I update my site's content later?
Yes. We deliver the full source code and can set up a CMS on request so you can update text and images without coding. Monthly maintenance plans are also available.
What are the payment terms?
50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and Stripe.
