Our Services

Clear plans, no fine print. Pick the one that fits, or tell us your idea and we'll build it together.

Important

Notes on Pricing

Lower range — client provides a design file (Figma, XD, or template). Design time is significantly reduced.
Upper range — design created from scratch (unique graphics, animations, or full branding).
Hourly rate of $40/hr available for work that doesn't fit a defined scope.
All sites are built mobile-responsive and cross-browser compatible unless otherwise agreed.
Prices do not include copywriting, photography, or stock image licensing.
Web accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA) available as an add-on — ask for a quote.
Legal

Terms of Service

Term Detail
Payment 50% upfront to start, 50% on delivery. Accepted via bank transfer, PayPal, or Stripe.
Late payment If the final balance is not settled within 7 business days of delivery, a 5% weekly fee applies to the outstanding amount.
Revisions Up to 2 rounds of revisions included. One round = one consolidated list of changes delivered in a single message. Additional rounds billed at $40/hr.
Scope Any features not listed above are considered separate projects with their own quote.
Timeline Estimates assume prompt feedback (within 48 hrs). Delays on the client's side may extend delivery.
Ownership Full rights transferred to client upon final payment.
Hosting Not included. Client is responsible for domain and hosting.
Maintenance Available at $40/hr or as a monthly retainer — ask for details.
Payment methods Bank transfer (ACH/wire), PayPal, Stripe (credit/debit card).
